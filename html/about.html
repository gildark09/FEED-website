About FEED

Welcome to FEED the smart automatic pet feeder created to make feeding your pets simple, reliable, and fully controllable from any device.

FEED allows you to manually feed your pet instantly with a single tap or schedule feeding times so your pet is fed automatically throughout the day — even if you're not home.

What FEED Offers

  • One-tap manual feeding for instant portions.
  • Automatic schedules for daily feeding times.
  • Remote control from any device, anywhere.
  • Custom portion sizes based on your pet’s needs.
  • Consistency and reliability for healthier pets.

With FEED, you get peace of mind knowing your pet is fed on time — even when you're away.
